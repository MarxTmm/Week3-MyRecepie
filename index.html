Pad Thai

4 portions

Ingridients Amount
Pad Thai sauce 150 g
Rice noodles 180 g
Chicken fillet 400 g
Cabbage 300 g
Carrot 1 pc
Egg 1 pc
Oil 2 tbs
Lime 1 pc
Peanuts 1 dl
Chilli flakes 1 tsp
  1. Cut and fry chicken fillet on the pan
  2. Chop and add cabbage to the pan
  3. Add lightly whisked egg
  4. Mix well
  5. Boil rice noodles in water for 3 minutes
  6. Cut noodles into smaller pieces and add to pan
  7. Add Pad Thai sauce
  8. Stir and fry the mix for few minutes
  9. Decorate with peanuts, lime and chilli flakes

Bon appetit!
